Sign In Lists

A sign in list can be used prior to the start of a half stage, to have competitors write their signature on.

Sign in lists can be generated through Sign in lists in the Generate menu. The sign in lists for all stages will be generated instantly then.

Only competitors that finished the previous half stage are stated on the sign in list.

After the process of signing in, the not signed in competitors can be processed as signed out, after which the final cross out lists can be generated.

Sign in lists are not listed in the event's table of contents.
