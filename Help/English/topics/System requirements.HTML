System Requirements

The following system requirements apply:

  • Pentium® or compatible PC with Microsoft® Windows® XP or any newer version;
  • 15Mb free disk space and CD-ROM drive;
  • Microsoft® Internet Explorer 4.0 or up must be installed, but Microsoft® Internet Explorer 5.0 or up is recommended;
  • Screen settings at 800×600 resolution or up, but 1024×768 resolution or up is recommended; and
  • Parallel port (can be in use already) or free USB port available.
