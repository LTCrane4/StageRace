Publication Settings

Through Settings... in the Configure menu, and then choosing for the Publication tab, you can lay down the exact contents that is printed on publications.

When the small size of the settings control holds you back from properly making the settings, you can maximize the control in which you make these settings, by double-clicking on any whitespace in the control.

 

Settings publication

 

The following publication settings can be made. These settings are among the most powerful that are available in StageRace.

Communique Numbering

You can enable the communiqué numbering system.

Header

You can fully configure the contents of the publication header:

  • Choose to print a recognition string in the top left corner of publications, and if it must be repeated on subsequent pages;
  • Choose to print the event title and subtitle and set its alignment;
  • Choose the alignment of the event logo;
  • Choose whether subtitles must be replaced or accompanied by the appropriate classification sponsors or that the sponsors must not be printed at all; and
  • Select the property labels that are printed at the top of publications and if labels and values are printed side-by-side or under each other.
Body

You can fully configure the columns for publications.

Note that here you can set the printing of higher accuracy that is used for time trials to be applied only when it involves two consecutive competitors having a draw in the full seconds time (2.4.016, 2.5.012) or even switch off higher accuracy times altogether, no matter it being a time trial.

Note that you can have competitors lists on teams be printed in a number of columns, selected between one and six.

You can set the token that is used as indicator instead of gap times that are equal to the previous ones in Gap for new time only columns.

Footer

You can fully configure the publication footer:

  • Enable printing of competitors finishing after the finish deadline in table form;
  • Enable printing of dropped out competitors on stage classifications in table form;
  • Enable printing of sign outs on stage classifications;
  • Enable printing of statistic information: general statistics, competitors after the finish deadline and dropped out competitors in compact form;
  • Enable and configure printing of the signature; and
  • Enable printing of a time stamp, with optional time zone information, and give its accuracy.

You can enter the text that is printed as signature. The entered signature can be name plus title of the chairman of the jury, for instance.

Languages

You can select the languages that are used on publications. Available languages are shown, and can be added, already previously added languages can be removed.

Cross Out Lists

The way cross out lists look can be chosen. You can choose to include team names, competitor names or both. For when team names are included, the amount of competitors on each row of the cross out list can be set.

Mask Indicators

You can enter the tokens that are used as indicator for a mask on the competitor lists.

For instance: # or U21, but HTML text like <IMG SRC="logo.gif" WIDTH="21"> is also allowed.

The choices made here only apply if the particular mask is activated in the general settings.

This can only be entered for masks 1, 2 and 3. The other masks will automatically be indicated as M4, M5, et cetera.

Miscellaneous

You can choose to use kilometres, miles or both. This also affects speeds. Note that this setting only applies to publications, not to exported files.

You can choose to print the table of contents in normal or reverse order, and where to print the references to competitors lists (top or bottom).

Advertisement

You can enable your own advertisement information at the bottom of each publication, rather than just a short StageRace one-liner, and choose if and how there is a hyperlink included, and enter the text for the advertisement. You can enter the name and one extra informative line about the party that is in charge of the classification service for the event.

This information will be printed as advertising at the bottom of all publications.
